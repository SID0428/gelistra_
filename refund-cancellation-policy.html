Refund and Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines cancellation and refund conditions for Gelistra service engagements.

Important Points

Effective date: March 29, 2026

  • Initial booking/advance amount is generally non-refundable.
  • Refunds depend on work completed up to cancellation date.
  • Custom development work already delivered is billable.
  • Send cancellation requests to hello@gelistra.com.

1. Project Cancellation by Client

Clients may request cancellation in writing. Cancellation becomes effective only after written confirmation from Gelistra.

2. Advance and Booking Fees

Advance amounts are used to reserve project slots, planning time, and initial execution. These are typically non-refundable once work has started.

3. Refund Eligibility

  • If no work has started, partial refund may be considered after deduction of processing/admin costs.
  • If work has started, refunds are calculated after deducting completed milestone value and resource costs.
  • If project is near completion or delivered, refund is generally not applicable.

4. Delay Due to Client Dependency

If delays occur because required content, approvals, or credentials are not provided on time, refund claims on that basis may not be accepted.

5. Cancellation by Gelistra

Gelistra reserves the right to cancel projects in exceptional cases such as policy violations, abusive conduct, or prolonged non-response. In such cases, fair settlement is made based on work completed.

6. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7 to 14 business days through the original payment channel where possible.
